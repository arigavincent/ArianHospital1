About Arian Hospital

Arian Hospital is a leading modern healthcare facility located in Kirinyaga, Kenya, dedicated to providing accessible, affordable, and high-quality medical care. Established to meet the growing healthcare needs of the community, Arian Hospital blends compassionate service with modern medical technology and professional expertise.

We take pride in offering a wide range of healthcare services including Emergency Care, Outpatient and Inpatient Services, Maternity & Child Care, Laboratory Diagnostics, Surgery, Pharmacy, and Radiology. Our team of experienced doctors, nurses, and specialists work together to ensure every patient receives personalized care in a safe and comfortable environment.

Our Vission

To be a center of medical excellence in Kenya and beyond, providing world-class healthcare services that improve the quality of life for all individuals.

Our Mission

To deliver comprehensive, compassionate, and patient-centered healthcare by combining advanced medical technology, professional expertise, and a commitment to integrity, empathy, and continuous improvement.
